A Mortgage Calculator

for the Kid in all of us

When you start looking for a house, you have a million questions, many of them circling around money. Specifically, you want to know how much you can afford. This kind of question is usually answered with a fanciful little form requiring you enter a bunch of variables. The problem with this is that you probably don't know what values to plug in yet. You just want to play around!

This form is that playground. It allows you to drag sliders and enter data. Maybe if you stretch with extra payments, the benefits of your hard work will pay off better than expected. Maybe a 15 year suits your goals better than a 30 year. Now you can see some "hard" numbers and figure out how to make this homeownership dream happen.

Each time you create a new result, it is automatically saved to the visual graph. You can clear the graph and start over if it's getting too cluttered. If you don't clear it, the last 100 results will be saved. The cool thing is that these results are saved to your browser's persistent storage, so until you clear your browsers site data, you will be able to return to this page and see all your data!
